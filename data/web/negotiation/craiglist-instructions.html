You and another user online will negotiate the price of a used car.

Instructions

  • Interface instructions
    • Your role (buyer or seller) is to the right, as well as the description of the used car.
    • Your expected price range is provided as well. We will award bonuses to those who get a better deal!
    • Use the chat box below to negotiate with your partner given the description on the right.
    • Be creative, but don't contradict anything given in the description!
    • When you and your partner have agreed on a price, enter it in the text box at right and hit 'Submit'.
    • You must agree on a final price within the time limit in order to receive credit for this HIT.
    • If you cannot reach a deal and do not complete the negotiation, we will award partial credit to the HIT if you made a good effort to negotiate.
