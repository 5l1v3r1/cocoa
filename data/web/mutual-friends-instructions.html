You and another user online have exactly one mutual friend in common. Your goal is to find that mutual friend!

Instructions

  • Please use natural sentences as much as possible.
    • Do: three of my friends went to ut austin
    • Don't do: 3 ut austin
  • Avoid simply listing out the names (or schools, majors, and companies) of your friends .
  • Look at your list of friends at right.
  • Use the chat box below to find out more about your partner's friends.
  • Once you've found out who it is, select them by clicking Select in the first column, next to their name.
  • Please select carefully. If you select a friend once, the website will not allow you to select another friend for 10 seconds.
